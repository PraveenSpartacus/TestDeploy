IOT Computer Lab

New Gen IEDC Rce-centre aim to support the student IoT based research. New Gen IEDC IoT Lab is a providing research platform exploring the potential of crowd sourcing and Internet of Things for multidisciplinary research with more end-user interactions. It gives the power to the crowd to be at the core of the research and innovation process. New Gen IEDC –Rce IoT lab facilitate to support the students at different Levels and Deployment Templates Device, Resource, Controller Service, Database, Web service, Analysis Component and Application.​

3D Printing Lab

New Gen IEDC-Rce established 3D Printing Lab. 3D printing also known as additive manufacturing, has become one of the key technologies in engineering, enabling complex geometric or organic shaped parts to be designed on a computer and built with a fully-automated manufacturing process. New Gen IEDC aim to enrich and facilitate the students to develop their skills in additive manufacturing technology to full fill their ideas​
